Data Desk

Welcome to Code for South Africa’s Data Desk. Our mission is simple: To make corporate data freely and publically available, and to empower you to hold the right people in the corporate sector accountable when they do things that affect your everyday life.

We believe you shouldn't have to pay for company information that should legally and legitimately be public.

Data Desk is a place where you can ask us to help you find information about corporates that affects you. We want to help you find it.

What is corporate data?

Corporate data can be defined in four ways, although we’d love to hear from you if there’s more we should be looking for.

  • Corporate structure. (i.e. corporate ownership, activity, key actors.)
  • The relationship between the company and the state (i.e. tender information, ownership, potential conflicts of interest)
  • The relationship between the company and its shareholders (i.e. shareholder structure, reported information, financial information)
  • The relationship between the company and communities (i.e. environmental impact, social responsibility, fair business practises.
