Welcome to Code for South Africa. Open Data. Civic Technology. Data Journalism.

Code for South Africa is a non-profit civic technology lab. We use data and technology to promote informed decision-making that drives social change. Our base of operations is Codebridge in Cape Town where we are helping to create a civic technology movement.
